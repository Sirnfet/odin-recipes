Lasagna Recipe

Description

This dish is amazing italian flat noodle covered with cheese and tomato sauce. It is layered with cheese, meat, then another cheese layer for a mouthful of flavor.

    Ingredients:
  • Flat Noodles
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Ground Beef
  • Mozzarella Cheese

    Steps to Cook:
  1. Boil Flat Noodles in water pot
  2. Cook Ground Beef till no more pink
  3. Lay cooked noodles in baking pan
  4. spread Mozzarella Cheese on bottom layer
  5. add another Flat Noodle
  6. spread cooked Ground beef
  7. Repeat desired amount of times
  8. Bake at 425 degrees for 20 minutes
